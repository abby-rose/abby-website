More About Me:

I'm currently pursuing my BS in Computer Science at Georgia Tech. My anticipated graduation date is Spring 2022 (but knowing Georgia Tech, I'll be here for longer). However, I'm honestly okay with that. Georgia Tech has truly given me so much: an incredible community, inspiring faculty and (most importantly of all) Blue Donkey Coffee. Throughout my time at Tech, I have been an orientation leader and been involved with Executive Round Table, Greek Sustainability Committee and Georgia Tech Tour Guides. Aside from school, I enjoy spending time with my friends since friends and community are incredibly important to me. I also enjoy running, making art for my hand calligraphy instagram @fancywritingbyabby and obsessing over Disney movies. I am frequently found studying at Blue Donkey Coffee or trying to convince my friends to go to Sweet Hut with me. Look through the pictures below to get a glimpse of what my life is like.
