FASET: Summer 2019

My experience as an orientation leader was one of the most rewarding parts of my college experience thus far. I was skeptical of the program coming in and even more skeptical as singing broke out on the bus ride to the all-leader retreat. However, I was blown away by the community that the FASET staff provided and how accepting everyone was of different types of people. I developed greater skills as a leader and become a more confident individual. I'm incredibly greatful for the opportunity to have served on FASET staff.
